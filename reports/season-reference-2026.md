## Glossary (for the report)

- **AVG / OBP / SLG** — batting average · on-base % · slugging %. **OPS** = OBP + SLG (one-number bat value; ~.750 is solid, .900+ is excellent).
- **ISO** (Isolated Power) = SLG − AVG — raw power independent of singles.
- **BB% / K%** — walk and strikeout rate per plate appearance (process/discipline).
- **L5 / L3** — totals over the last 5 games (bat) / 3 outings (pitch); the recent-form window.
- **Trend** — last-5 AVG minus season AVG, in points (▲ hot / ▼ cold). Filters small-sample noise from real swings.
- **ERA / WHIP** — earned runs per 9 IP · baserunners (H+BB) per IP. **K/9, BB/9** — strikeouts and walks per 9 IP (lower BB/9 = better command).
- **Role** — SP starter · RP reliever · SwingP both, inferred from games-started share.

## How to use this in a post-game report

- **Frame each line vs. the season baseline** above (e.g. "2-for-4 raises X from .250 → .268" or "the 5 BB matches a season-long command theme").
- **Cite the Trend column** to call a player genuinely hot/cold vs. small-sample noise.
- **Pitching:** compare the night's strike% and BB to the staff BB/9 baseline; flag arms whose L3 ERA diverges from season ERA.
- **Regenerate** after the seed refreshes: `node scripts/season-report.js`.
